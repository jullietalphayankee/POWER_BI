# 💳 Credit Card Transaction Report (Power BI Dashboard)

A powerful, interactive Power BI dashboard visualizing credit card usage, customer behavior, and revenue metrics to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

| Metric              | Value      |
|---------------------|------------|
| Total Revenue        | ₹55M       |
| Total Transaction Amt| ₹45M       |
| Total Interest Earned| ₹8M        |
| Total Transactions   | 656K       |

---

## 🔍 Insights

- 🟦 **Blue Cardholders** are the most profitable, contributing ₹46M revenue.
- 🕒 **Q3** shows peak performance in both revenue and transaction volume.
- 🛍️ **Bills, Entertainment**, and **Fuel** are top spending areas.
- 👨‍💼 **Businessmen** and **Graduates** drive the most transactions.
- 💳 **Swipe** mode dominates over **Chip** and **Online** payments.

---

## 🎯 Features

- **Quarter, Week, Gender, Card Type, Income** filters
- Interactive visuals: bar charts, line graphs, slicers
- Dynamic KPIs and segmented revenue breakdowns
- Data grouped by:
  - **Expenditure Type**
  - **Education Level**
  - **Customer Job**
  - **Transaction Method**
  - **Card Category**
